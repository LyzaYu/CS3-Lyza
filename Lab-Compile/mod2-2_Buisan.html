Hunter x Hunter

The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss. His father left him at a young age and he discoves that he was a "hunter", because of this he is now aiming to become a great hunter. Through this journey he meets many people and friends. As he gets stronger and stronger he also meets more opponents.

Characters

  2. Killua Zoldyck
  3. Isaac Netro
